Delete Data — Sober Tracker

Last updated: May 1, 2026

How to delete your data in the app

Sober Tracker lets you delete your local app data directly from inside the app.

Steps:

  • Open Sober Tracker
  • Go to Settings
  • Tap Delete all my data
  • Review the warning message
  • Confirm deletion

What is deleted

When you use Delete all my data, the app deletes local app data stored on your device, including:

  • tracker names
  • sobriety start dates
  • check-ins, notes, and mood scores
  • milestone progress
  • personal reasons ("My Why")
  • reminder and app preference data

The app then resets to its initial onboarding state.

What is not deleted

Some records are managed by third parties and are not deleted by the in-app delete flow, including:

  • purchase history held by Apple or Google
  • subscription and receipt records managed by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat
  • crash reports previously processed by Firebase Crashlytics, subject to Firebase retention policies

No account required

Sober Tracker does not require an account. There is no separate account profile to close.
