Privacy Policy — Sober Tracker

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Overview

Sober Tracker is a local-first, privacy-focused sobriety tracking app. We believe your sobriety journey is private, and we've designed the app to keep it that way.

No analytics. No tracking. No account required. You can use the app without creating an account, and your personal tracking data stays on your device unless you explicitly export it.

What We Collect

Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

We collect anonymous crash reports only to maintain app stability and fix bugs. This is the only data that leaves your device besides purchase receipts.

Crash reports include:

  • Stack traces (technical error information)
  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) — app stability is essential for user safety and product quality.

What crash reports do NOT include:

  • ❌ Your check-in notes
  • ❌ Your sobriety dates
  • ❌ Your mood scores
  • ❌ Your personal reasons ("My Why")
  • ❌ Any user-generated content

Important: We intentionally do not include any user-generated text in crash reports. If the app crashes while you're writing a note, that note is never transmitted.

Purchase Information (RevenueCat / Apple / Google)

When you purchase a Pro subscription, payment processing is handled by Apple or Google. RevenueCat manages entitlement state on our behalf. This includes:

  • Purchase receipts
  • Subscription status

Legal basis: Contract performance (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)) — necessary to provide the Pro features you purchased.

What We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what we never collect:

  • ❌ No analytics of any kind — We removed all analytics SDKs. Zero tracking.
  • ❌ No usage data — We do not monitor screen views, taps, session length, or feature usage
  • ❌ No personal data — We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • ❌ No health data — We do not collect, interpret, or transmit any health-related information
  • ❌ No location data — We do not track your location
  • ❌ No advertising identifiers — We do not use advertising IDs for any purpose
  • ❌ No third-party tracking — No Facebook Pixel, no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no PostHog

Version 1.2.2 change: We previously used PostHog for optional usage analytics (opt-in only). As of v1.2.2, we have removed PostHog entirely. The app now collects zero behavioral data.

Where Your Data Lives

On Your Device (Local Only)

All your personal tracking data is stored only on your device:

  • Sobriety start dates
  • Check-in notes and mood scores
  • Milestone progress
  • Personal reasons ("My Why")
  • Reminder settings
  • Tracker names

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

On Our Servers

We do not operate any servers that store your personal tracking data.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — View all your data in the app or request an export
  • Right to rectification — Edit any of your data directly in the app
  • Right to erasure — Use "Delete all my data" in Settings to erase everything
  • Right to data portability — Use "Export my data" in Settings to download your data as JSON
  • Right to object — Crashlytics operates under legitimate interest; you may object by contacting us
  • Right to lodge a complaint — With your local supervisory authority

Data Export

You can export all your data at any time using the "Export my data" feature in Settings. The export includes:

  • All trackers (names, start dates)
  • All milestones
  • All check-ins (notes, mood scores, dates)

Important: Once exported, the file leaves the app's secure storage and you are responsible for protecting it.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Delete all my data" feature in Settings. This:

  • ✅ Deletes all trackers, milestones, and check-ins
  • ✅ Clears all app preferences
  • ✅ Resets the app to its initial state
  • ⚠️ Cannot delete purchase history held by Apple or Google (this is outside our control)

Children's Privacy

Sober Tracker is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security

  • All personal tracking data is stored locally on your device
  • Crash reports are transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • No user-generated content is ever included in crash reports
  • The app includes a privacy overlay that hides content in the app switcher

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at contact@sobertracker.simplelogin.com.
